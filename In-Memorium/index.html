In Memorium of
Lost Dictionary Words

  • brabble: paltry noisy quarrel
  • growlery: place to growl in, private room, den
  • snowbroweth: freshly melted snow
  • twattle: to gossip
  • halier: a former monetary unit of Slovakia, equal to one hundredth of a korun
  • glocalization: the practice of conducting business according to both local and global considerations
  • cript kiddie: a person who uses existing scripts or codes to hack into computers, lacking the expertise to write their own
  • threequel: the third film, book, event, etc. in a series; a second sequel
  • curglaff: a scream brought on by plunging into cold water
  • jollux: a fat person
  • younker: a youngster
  • supererogate: to do more than duty requires
  • charabanc: a large bus used on sightseeing tours, especially one with open sides and no center aisle
  • frigorific: causing or producing cold
  • cockalorum: a little man who has a high opinion
  • lunting: to go for a walk while smoking a pipe
