MinARal
Experience Design for the Smithsonian
MinARal is a single-player, augmented reality based scavenger hunt game for mobile devices that educates young users on the real-world use and purpose of gems and minerals. Proposed to The Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History, our student team wanted to use adventure via augmented reality and whimsy with our story to ultimately get children excited about the science of mineralogy and a potential future in STEM during their museum visit.
THE
GOAL
Introducing Augmented
Reality to the Museum
In order to breathe new life into museum objects already on display, The Smithsonian partnered with Parsons to leverage our knowlege of new media and technology. Acting as a potential contractor, our student group offered the the museum a breadth of technical and creative concepts revolved around the augmented reality space in order to give their legacy exhibit, Gems & Minerals, a more contemporary appeal.
